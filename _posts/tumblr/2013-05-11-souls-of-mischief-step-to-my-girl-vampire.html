Souls of Mischief - Step to my Girl

Vampire Weekend’s song Step off their upcoming album Modern Vampires of the City pays a playful homage to this originally unreleased Souls of Mischief track. The track, which was on a demo that earned the band a record deal with Jive, was not released until 2003 due to issues with sample rights. HipHopDX has an interview with Tajai from the group, discussing his feelings on the homage.
